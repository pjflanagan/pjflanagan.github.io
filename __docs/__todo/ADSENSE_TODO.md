The Real "Thin Content" Issues

AdSense doesn't have a specific word count threshold, but they flag:

Posts likely to be flagged (~10-15 of yours):
  - Several posts under 300 words:
    - "Flanny App Redesign" (250)
    - "Wedding Website" (280)
    - "Garmin Asset Generator" (280),
    - "Tasty But Too Close" (280),
    - "Metrocard Wireless Charger" (300)
  - Project posts that are mostly a description + link, without much explanation of how, why, or what you learned

What Would Actually Help

1. Add an About page — this is the #1 thing AdSense reviewers look for
2. Expand the thin posts — for posts under 400 words, add context: what problem it solved, technical challenges, what you'd do differently
3. The draft (how-to-make-a-garmin-watch-face.md) — publish it; tutorial/how-to content is exactly what AdSense rewards
4. Quality over quantity — you don't need more posts, you need the existing short ones to have more substance

The site has good bones — 16 years of history, real projects, some long-form content. The gap is the short project-description posts and missing infrastructure pages, not total volume.
