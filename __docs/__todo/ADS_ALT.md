# Alternatives to Google AdSense for flanny.app

Based on the developer-focused, utility-heavy, and long-running nature of the flanny.app ecosystem, here are the best alternatives to Google AdSense.

## 1. Developer & Tech Focused (Minimalist & Pro)
These are ideal for utility apps (like `ohword`, `runcalculator`, `canvas`) because they are non-intrusive and respect user privacy.

*   **Carbon Ads:** The industry standard for developer sites. Shows a single, high-quality, minimalist ad.
    *   *Best for:* Homepage and app sidebars.
*   **EthicalAds:** Run by the "Read the Docs" team. Focuses heavily on privacy and developer-centric content.
    *   *Best for:* Technical blog posts and documentation-style content.

## 2. Revenue-Optimized (AI & Data Driven)
If the primary goal is to maximize RPM (Revenue Per Mille) from long-form blog content.

*   **Ezoic:** Uses AI to test different ad placements. Their "Access Now" program is available for smaller sites.
    *   *Best for:* High-traffic blog posts.
*   **Mediavine:** High payouts and excellent support, but requires 50,000 sessions per month.
    *   *Goal:* Aim for this if traffic scales.

## 3. Native & Community-Based
Less "ad-like" and more focused on the personal brand of the creator.

*   **Buy Me a Coffee / Ko-fi:** Add a simple "Support my projects" button.
    *   *Best for:* Utility apps where users find high value (e.g., `yes-chef`, `runcalculator`).
*   **Amazon Associates:** Use affiliate links for specific hardware or tools mentioned in posts (e.g., Keyboards, Garmin Mods).
    *   *Best for:* Hardware-focused blog posts.

## 4. Niche & Independent
*   **AdClerks:** Sell ad space directly to advertisers at your own price.
*   **Sovrn //Commerce (formerly VigLink):** Automatically converts product mentions into affiliate links.

## Comparison Table

| Network | Vibe | Best For | Traffic Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Carbon Ads** | Minimalist/Pro | Apps & Dev Blog | High-quality content (vetted) |
| **EthicalAds** | Privacy-first | Technical content | None (vetted for content) |
| **Ezoic** | Data-driven | Maximizing $ | Low (Access Now program) |

## Recommendations for flanny.app
1.  **Phase 1 (Immediate):** Add a **Buy Me a Coffee** link to the footer of all apps and the blog.
2.  **Phase 2 (Content):** Add **Amazon Affiliate** links to hardware posts (Keyboards, Garmin).
3.  **Phase 3 (Application):** Apply for **Carbon Ads** for the main homepage and utility apps once content is polished (per `ADSENSE_TODO.md`).
