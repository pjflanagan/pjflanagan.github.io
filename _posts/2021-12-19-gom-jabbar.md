One of my goals is for a website I make to have a wide reach, even if that's just for a little while. This was my first attempt at capitalizing on pop culture. It was a fun, silly thing to make, but I don't think that many people saw it.

Just put your cursor into the box, the background will flash with images of pain and Dune movies. Keep it in long enough and you'll survive, pull your hand out and you die.
